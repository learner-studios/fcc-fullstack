Basic Info: November 30, 1955 born and dies November 10, 2022 from battling cancer

Raised in New York (Westbury, NY and New York City)


Batman's performance from Kevin has been multi-layerd and a product not only of the roles that he shouldered but a product of time either as he moved further in his canon year's or even in the past when he was just starting out. 

Primarily raised in a conservative christian family he grew up primarily on the east coast,  by the age of 17 he had decided to pursue professionally an acting career.  

Starting off with performing in Broadway, he eventually was able to get accepted into Juliard and worked with fellow actors such as Robin Williams.  

Worked directly under John Houseman and toured with his company The Acting Company after graduating in 1979. 

Under the acting company he was able to play a part in such plays like Mid Summer Nights Dream, Hamlet, and King Lear.

In 1985 he shifted his acting craft to television and wa able to audition and perform for a role in the Batman Animated series that was coming up his first according to the "Inside of You" podcast, his original audition was for the role of Harvey Bullock.

At this point his only experience with Batman was with the Adam West series that appeared in he 1960s, with a lot of crafting however he was able to create the iconic voice we all know today.

His primary roles that he has been known for have almost exclusively been caught and recorded as Batman.
